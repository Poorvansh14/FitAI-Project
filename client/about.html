About
Our Mission.

At FitAI, we believe that fitness should be personalized, intelligent, and accessible to everyone. We're a team of fitness experts, data scientists, and developers dedicated to building an AI platform that adapts to your unique body, goals, and lifestyle.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to make professional-grade fitness guidance available to all. We saw the potential of artificial intelligence to not only create plans but to understand a person's individual needs, habits, and progress. The result is a system that grows with you, providing a truly dynamic and effective fitness partner.

Meet the Makers

This project was made possible by Gayatri Malukani, Urva Shah, and Poorvansh Patel.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: We are constantly exploring new technologies and scientific research to push the boundaries of what is possible in personal fitness.
  • Personalization: We believe a one-size-fits-all approach doesn't work. Our platform is built to understand and adapt to the unique needs of every individual user.
  • Transparency: We are committed to being open and honest about how our AI works, giving you a clear understanding of your plans and progress.
